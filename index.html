Miguel Young de la Sota

About

I'm Miguel, a graduate of the Massachussetts Institute of Technology. I majored in pure mathematics (what we call Course 18), with a view towards algebraic topology. Besides topology, I'm interested in distrbuted systems, computer security at scale, and practical computer language and compiler design.

While at MIT, I was Chair of the Student Information Processing Board, MIT's computing society. I spend a lot of time thinking about how to improve Rust, a language with interesting ideas about what can be checked by the comopiler. I like to think of myself as an artist, mostly analog ink and pencil, digital painting, and TeX typesetting.
